Opening Note:

Today, whether we sit surrounded by friends or enjoy a better place in life after surviving our worst phases, we must remember the ones who stood by us. There is always someone who sacrificed their time, effort, and peace just to see us reach where we are today. Such people never hesitated to help, never asked for anything in return—only wished for our happiness and success. Let us never forget them, never repay them with hurt, but instead honor their efforts with the same care and loyalty.

1. My Regret

My greatest regret is that during your worst phase, I gave everything I had—my time, my energy, my peace—just to see you in a better place, but in return I was left unvalued. I regret that even though I stood with you in every storm, when it was my turn to lean on you, you were not there for me. I regret ignoring my own needs and pain, believing that my sacrifices would one day be recognized, but instead, they were overlooked.

2. What I Did for You

During your darkest moments, I dedicated myself to lifting you up. I put in every ounce of effort to make your life better, to remind you that brighter days would come. I never hesitated to reply to your messages, even when I was busy or exhausted. I stayed available whenever you needed me, no matter how inconvenient it was for me.

I constantly tried to motivate you when you were on the verge of giving up, repeating again and again that things would eventually get better. I fought for you as if your battles were my own, standing like a shield when you were about to fall apart. Even when you doubted yourself, I assured you that following my advice would help you push through.

There were many times when you ended our friendship with harsh behavior, yet I was always the one who tried to convince you to come back. And when you did return, often with an emotional tone, I accepted you again and started everything from the beginning—despite the fact that most of the mistakes were yours.

3. How I Felt Hurt

What hurt me most was not just the mistakes, but the way you never stood by me when I truly needed you. I ignored the pain you gave me, hoping one day you would see how much I cared, but that day never came. For five years, I overlooked your mistakes and endured your emotional swings, sacrificing my own mental peace just to keep the bond alive.

It broke me to see that you had time for traveling, for spending moments with others, and for everything else in your life—but you didn't have time for me, the one who stood by you during your lowest phases. I gave you unconditional support, yet I was left without the same care in return. Despite contributing to the person you are today and the happiness you now enjoy, my role was ignored, and I was left feeling unseen and unappreciated.

4. What This Means to Me

This entire experience has left me questioning the meaning of loyalty and sacrifice. I don't understand why caring for someone so deeply only brought me pain and emptiness. It feels unfair that my devotion, my sleepless nights, my endless patience, and my sacrifices were never acknowledged. I am left wondering if helping someone and caring at such a level is even worth it, when all it seems to bring back is silence and regret.

5. Closing Reflection

Still, the truth remains: I was there. I stood by you in your worst phases, I gave you my time, my strength, and my love when you were about to give up. That will always remain a part of me, no matter what. Yet the wound of being unseen and unvalued cuts deep.

From this, I have learned a painful but important truth: giving everything to someone does not mean they will give the same in return. But it does reveal my own strength—my loyalty, my honesty, and my ability to care beyond limits. And even if I was left hurt, I know I loved and cared with all my heart, and that is something I will never regret about myself.
